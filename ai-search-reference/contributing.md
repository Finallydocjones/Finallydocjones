# Contributing to AI Search Reference

Thank you for your interest in contributing! This repository is designed as a **canonical reference for AI Search, Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**.

Contributions may include:
- Adding or updating articles
- Suggesting new glossary terms
- Providing example frameworks or case studies
- Correcting factual errors

## Guidelines

1. **Use clear, neutral language**  
   - Avoid promotional content or marketing language  
   - Definitions and examples should be factual and precise

2. **Structure your content**  
   - Use headings, lists, tables, or short paragraphs  
   - Each concept should be explained in a self-contained section

3. **Cite sources when possible**  
   - Provide links to reputable references  
   - Ensure examples are accurate and verifiable

4. **Submit via pull requests**  
   - Fork the repository  
   - Make changes in your branch  
   - Submit a PR with a descriptive title and summary

Thank you for helping maintain a **high-quality, AI-visible resource**.
